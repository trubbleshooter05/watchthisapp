## What to look for in a true Interstellar match

When you evaluate sci-fi follow-ups, weight these signals:

1. **Time as a character** — stories where relativity, memory, or distance changes relationships.
2. **Awe without irony** — films that let wonder breathe instead of undercutting every beat.
3. **Hard-ish science texture** — orbital mechanics, AI ethics, or ecological collapse with internal logic.
4. **Emotional payoff** — the ending should *cost* something, not just explode prettily.
