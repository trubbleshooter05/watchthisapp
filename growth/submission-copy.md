1. movieslike.app is an AI-powered movie discovery platform focused on one core job: helping people quickly find movies similar to what they already love. Instead of endless scrolling, users search a title and land on focused "movies like [title]" pages with recommendation match scores, concise explanations, and genre/vibe context. The homepage also supports genre-first discovery (romcom, thriller, action, etc.) so users can browse by mood before picking a film. The product is free, requires no login, and is optimized for fast decision-making.

2. movieslike.app turns "What should I watch next?" into a 30-second task. Enter a movie, get strong alternatives, and browse recommendations through a clean, visual interface. Each recommendation page is structured for practical discovery with quality summaries, mood-aware framing, and easy navigation to related picks. Users can start by title or by genre, making the experience useful for both targeted and exploratory browsing. No account is required, and the site is designed to reduce choice overload.

3. movieslike.app is a lightweight movie recommendation engine for fans who want relevance without friction. It combines AI-assisted recommendation generation with structured movie-like pages, then layers on fast filters and genre discovery to improve watch intent. The platform is free, no-login, and built with a focus on usability over novelty. If someone likes a movie and wants immediate, believable alternatives, this tool provides a fast answer.
